About Family Weather

Family Weather exists for one simple reason: real people plan real life around the weather.

Most weather apps are built for doom-scrolling storms. Family Weather is built for parents, families, coaches, and groups who just need to know: Can we do the thing?

  • Plan events with forecast + historical context
  • Share once, keep everyone informed
  • Reduce last-minute cancellations and awkward “should we cancel?” texts

This started as a family tool and is growing the same way: intentionally, practically, and with respect for people’s time.
